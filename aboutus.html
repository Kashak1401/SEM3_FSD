About Us

Welcome to TheChocoTales – where every chocolate has a story to tell

At TheChocoTales, we believe chocolate is a heavenly gift. With its irresistible allure, chocolate offers a spectrum of flavors: sweet, bitter, bittersweet, and sweet-sour, each a unique and exquisite delight. Inspired by the Greek term for cocoa, ‘theobroma’, meaning “food of the gods,” we aptly named our brand ‘Manna’.

Born in the Heart of India’s Cocoa Haven

TheChocoTales started in the lush highlands of Kerala, the prime cocoa-growing region of India. We meticulously select only the finest cocoa beans and ingredients, ensuring every bite of Macofa Homemade Chocolate reflects our dedication and passion. Our commitment to perfection drives us to continually craft new flavors with love and care.

Our Chocolate Boutiques

  • Chocolate Factory in Munnar, Kerala: Nestled in the scenic beauty of Munnar, our factory is where the magic begins.
  • Chocolate Kitchen in Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu: Located in the serene hills of Kodaikanal, our kitchen is a haven for chocolate lovers.

Signature Creations Now Online

Our signature creations, each uniquely wrapped, are now available through our online store. Perfect for every festive occasion, our luxurious delights include rich dark chocolates, flavored treats, and creamy white chocolates. We’ve mastered the art of creating unique wonders that bring pleasure to your taste buds.

Nationwide Shipping and a Rising Brand

TheChocoTales offers free delivery across India. As one of the fastest-growing chocolate brands in India and a leading name in South India, we bring the finest chocolate experiences to our customers.
